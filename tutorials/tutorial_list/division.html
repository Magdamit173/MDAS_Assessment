Basic Division Tutorial

Welcome to the Basic Division Tutorial! In this tutorial, we'll cover the fundamentals of division.

Division is the process of splitting a quantity into equal parts or groups.

Dividing Positive Numbers

To divide positive numbers, simply split the total quantity into the specified number of equal parts. For example: 


    6 ÷ 2 = 3
    12 ÷ 3 = 4
    20 ÷ 5 = 4
    15 ÷ 5 = 3

Dividing Negative Numbers

Dividing negative numbers can result in a positive or negative quotient depending on the signs of the numbers.

  • Positive Divided by Positive is equal to Positive
  • Negative Divided by Negative is equal to Positive
  • Positive Divided by Negative is equal to Negative, And vice versa

    • For Example: 

    
    -6 ÷ 2 = -3
    12 ÷ -3 = -4
    -20 ÷ -5 = 4
    -15 ÷ 5 = -3
