Basic Multiplication Tutorial

Welcome to the Basic Multiplication Tutorial! In this tutorial, we'll cover the fundamentals of multiplication.

Multiplication is the process of repeated addition. It's a way of adding the same number together multiple times.

Multiplying Positive Numbers

To multiply positive numbers, simply add the number to itself the specified number of times. For example: 


    2 x 3 = 6
    4 x 5 = 20
    7 x 8 = 56
    10 x 2 = 20

Multiplying Negative Numbers

Multiplying negative numbers can result in a positive or negative product depending on the number of negative factors.

  • Positive Multiplied by Positive is equal to Positive
  • Negative Multiplied by Negative is equal to Positive
  • Positive Multiplied by Negative is equal to Negative, And vice versa

    • For Example: 

    
    -2 x 3 = -6
    4 x -5 = -20
    -7 x -8 = 56
    -10 x -2 = 20
