Basic Addition Tutorial

Welcome to the Basic Addition Tutorial! In this tutorial, we'll cover the fundamentals of addition.

Addition is the process of combining two or more numbers to find their total sum.

Adding Positive Numbers

To add positive numbers, simply combine the numbers together. For example: 


    1 + 1 = 2
    3 + 4 = 7
    7 + 4 = 11
    6 + 9 = 14

Adding Negative Numbers

To add negative numbers it involves subtracting. When you add a negative number, you decrease the total. For example: 


    -1 + 1 = 0
    -5 + 3 = -2
    4 + (-7) = -3
    -3 + (-3) = -6
