Basic Subtraction Tutorial

Welcome to the Basic Subtraction Tutorial! In this tutorial, we'll cover the fundamentals of subtraction.

Subtraction is the process of finding the difference between two numbers.

Subtracting Positive Numbers

To subtract positive numbers, simply take away the second number (subtrahend) from the first number (minuend). For example: 


    3 - 1 = 2
    7 - 4 = 3
    -4 - 6 = -10
    similar: -4 + (-6) = -10

Subtracting Negative Numbers

Subtracting negative numbers involves adding. When you subtract a negative number, you increase the total. For example: 


    1 - (-1) = 2
    3 - (-2) = 5
    8 - (-4) = 12
    -3 - (-4) = 1
    similar: -3 + 4 = 1
